Importance of Email Lists ğŸ¯ğŸ’ŒğŸ“«

Getting our beautiful content in front of someone's face is getting harder by the day. Emails are still one of the greatest tools into the lives of prospective leads.

Cold emailing can work, but how about getting a lead to engage with you AND hand deliver their email address?

Unfortunately, it takes more than a simple text input on a website to capture a lead's email address.

People are looking for value in exchange for a key to their personal inbox, or as I like to call it, their home.

You need to offer something in return, such as:
—Free e-books or tips
—Free trials or products
—Coupon codes
—Newsletters/podcasts

Try to get creative with it.

And make sure you don't get lost in spam :)
